1. Introduction to Software Engineering
 what is SW engineering?
  Software engineering is an engineering discipline which is concerned with all aspects of software production.
 
 Essential software
  maintainability, dependability and security, efficiency and acceptabillity
 
 The high-level activities
  specification, development, validation and evolution

 The fundamental notions
  universally applicable to all types of system development

 Software engineers have responsibilities to the engineering  profession and society.
